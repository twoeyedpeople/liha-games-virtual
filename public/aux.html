Operations Guide

Quick operational flow for managers running live sessions.

Before Participants Join

  1. Open Control and sign in with the master key.
  2. Confirm both sessions (A and B) are in the expected state.
  3. Launch Recruiter Focus Finder, single Leaderboard, Trust Who Countdown, and Soft Skills Significance Countdown on venue displays.
  4. Use Claim Leaderboard on Control to point the single leaderboard display to Group A or Group B.

During Trust Who

  1. Start Trust Who for the session in control.
  2. Mark each team as finished when they complete the activity.
  3. Watch countdown results and confirm points are applied.

During Soft Skills Significance

  1. Run Soft Skills Significance as 3 manual rounds of 2 minutes each from control.
  2. Mark each team as finished when they complete the activity.
  3. Watch each round timer/results, then confirm cumulative points are applied after Round 3.

During Prompt Like a Pro

  1. Open lobby and share the current passcode.
  2. Launch round once team participation is ready.
  3. Review round results and merged leaderboard output.

After the Event

  1. Review aggregated performance in Stats.
  2. Export spreadsheet report via XLS.
  3. Reset sessions for the next cohort when required.
